About Online Harassment

The internet is an incredible platform for talking with friends, developing cool things, and for spending a vast number of hours watching Netflix. However, Some use the internet to spread hatred and unkind words. Respect others the way that you would like to be respected. If you see someone be harassed, Take Action . For more info, go to HackHarassment.

Statistics

  • "Fully 73% of adult internet users have seen someone be harassed in some way online and 40% have personally experienced it."(Pew Research Center)
  • "Among those who have experienced online harassment, 60% decided to ignore their most recent incident while 40% took steps to respond to it."(Pew Research Center)
  • "In a 2012 survey, 89 percent of local domestic violence programs reported that victims were experiencing intimidation and threats by abusers via technology, including through cell phones, texts, and email."(NNEDV)
  • "1 in 6 online teens say they have been contacted online by someone they did not know in a way that made them feel scared or uncomfortable."(Pew Internet and American Life Project, 2013)
